ACME Company was founded in 2002 when three friends decided they wanted to change the world for the better. They knew that a world without magic was too plain and boring, and so they set out to make the world awesome. Even though they knew creating magic was the hardest problem any of them have faced, it did not stop them from making the impossible possible. 11 years into the process, and they have nearly figured it out. It's this determination, persistence, and passion that has led to their near-success of the creation of magic.
